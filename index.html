Everything Your Club Needs

For Coaches

📋

Drill Library & Builder

Create drills with an interactive pitch diagram builder, attach media and control visibility across your squads. Unlock premium drill packs to elevate your sessions.

🏃

Player Profiles

Full player records with skills, positions, shirt numbers, attendance and performance history, giving you everything you need to track individual development.

📅

Training Sessions

Plan and schedule sessions, assign drills from your library, and track player attendance, all in one place.

⚽

Match Management

Sync your match schedule with popular league platforms such as Dribl, then set your formation & line-up with an intuitive interface. Prepare pre-game tactics and post-game notes and communication to keep your squad aligned before, during and after every game.

🎙️

Match Recording

Record key match stats (e.g. goals, assists, saves, substitutions, etc.) in real time, synced to the match clock. Supporters can follow the action live, so parents never miss a moment, even when they can't be there.

📊

Squad Stats & Leaderboards

Track goals, assists, minutes played, attendance and more. Leaderboards keep players motivated and give coaches an instant performance snapshot.

💬

Team Communication

Role-based access and secure invite codes ensure the right information reaches the right people: coaches, players and supporters.

For Club Admins

🖥️

Training Console

Your club's coaching philosophy, methodology and player development pathways should be assets the club owns, not knowledge that walks out the door when a coach moves on. The Training Console gives Club Admins a central command centre: a shared drill library, a structured training plan repository, and full visibility across every squad's sessions and matches. Push training plans to any squad or your entire club at once, standardise your coaching methodology from grassroots to senior level, and track player development with confidence. When coaches change, your IP stays.

🔑

Licensing & User Management

Grant access to individual users and assign them as Club Admins, Squad Coaches or Player Supporters under your club-wide licence, keeping your club's data secure and your team properly structured.

👥

Squad Access

Step into any squad within your club and operate with the full suite of squad coach tools, ideal for providing hands-on support, reviewing how a squad is running or covering a session.

For Supporters

👨‍👩‍👧

Supporter Access

Stay close to your child's football journey, no matter how busy life gets. Follow their individual progress, view training sessions, match appearances and live match recordings, all tailored specifically to your child so you're always in the loop, even when you can't make it to the sideline.

Club Licensing

Managing multiple squads across your club? SquadManager Pro is available as a club-wide licence, giving every coach access to the full feature set under a single arrangement. With a shared drill library, club-level training plan rollouts and centralised oversight across all your squads and age groups.
